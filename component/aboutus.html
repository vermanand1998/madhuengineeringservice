About Company

Ihita Industries Private Limited is one of the market leaders of Manufacturing in Steel and Aluminium Products.

Since 2014,We are the leading suppliers of Steel and Aluminium Fabrication components to Engineering Sectors. Our Company Ihita Industries Pvt Ltd is an ISO 9001:2015 certified organisation and we assures 100% satisfaction in customer needs and their requirements. We have Prime Factory spaces in Madurai and our manufacturing area consists around 25000 Sq feet and we are in the process of expansion. We offer a one stop end to end solution for our customers, ranging from Raw Material purchase, to Production for all engineering sectors like Automobiles,Construction,Solar and Other Industrials..

History

Ihita Industries is a private Ltd group consist of two companies, delivering Service and Engineering to the Construction and Industrial market. Ihita group was established in the year 2010 started in the name of Ihita Engineering Services in Chennai, which is in the construction field which plays a major role in Design and Services for Aluminium formwork systems In the year 2014, Ihita Industries Pvt Ltd started our own manufacturing unit as “Ihita Industries“ as a partnership firm with the young team of Engineers at Chennai especially for the fabrication of Aluminium and Steel components for Aluminium formwork systems. In the year 2015, We shifted our production unit to Madurai and we expanded our Production shop floor area from 2500 sq feet to 10000 sq feet. Our Plant centrally located in SIDCO industrial area, Kappalur, Madurai by connecting both NH45 and NH 7 highways for easy access. In the year 2017, We expanded our production area shop floor from 10000 sq feet to 20000 Sq feet. From 2017, We started supplying engineering products to both the Industrial and Construction markets with highly efficient machinery and manpower.
