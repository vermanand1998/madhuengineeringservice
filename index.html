About Company

Since 2014,We are the leading suppliers of Steel and Aluminium Fabrication components to Engineering Sectors. Our Services

SHEET METAL SHEARING

Ihita Industries is the leading Service Providers of Steel and Aluminium Shearing Services in Madurai, Tamil Nadu. Shearing processes is a process in which a piece of sheet metal is separated by applying a great enough force to bigger length and width sheets. The most common cutting processes are performed by applying a shearing force and referred to as shearing processes.
